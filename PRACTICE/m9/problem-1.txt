Given string is; " E S A Y Q U E S T I O N "

  First we consider low , mid, high index elements as reference in merge sort. Then divide the given array into subarray; from n/2 to 0 way, as shown below steps.

 1. E A S Y Q U | E S T I O N

 2. E A S | Y Q U || E S T | I O N

 3. E A | S || Y Q | U ||| E S | T || I O | N

 4. E / A | S || Y / Q | U ||| E / S | T || I / O | N

 5. A E | S || Q Y | U ||| E S | T || I O | N

 6. A E S || Q U Y ||| E S T || I N O

 7. A E Q S U Y ||| E I N O S T
 
 8. A E E I N O Q S S T U Y

 Therefore sorted form of the given array is;  " A E E I N O Q S S T U Y ".
